DESIGN PRINCIPLES

Axa Uribe

PROPORTION

YouTube

www.youtube.com

YouTube is a perfect example of balanced delivery that positively influences the user experience. The application is divided into reels, publications and general videos sections. It is easy to navigate between them and the colors and sizes of each division are in harmony with the rest of the page.

REPETITION

Disney+

www.disneyplus.com

For the most part, streaming video platforms are one of the most consistent when it comes to the use of "replay". In the case of Disney+ we can see that the home page already provides us with a specific layout based on squares (vertical rectangles) containing the titles of the movies or series to offer. In the same way, if we search for a specific series through the search bar, the results will be organized in the same way. This makes the user feel comfortable and quickly access the information they need.

CONSISTENCY

Instagram

www.instagram.com

Consistency goes hand in hand with design, something that is not consistent is usually not appealing to the eye. I think Instagram is a great example. We have completely specific Tabs. The one for chats, the one for posts, and the one for reels. When you navigate through them, it makes it very easy to find your way around because they have the same basic functions and allows the user to engage with the social networking experience.
