PROPORTION

YouTube

www.youtube.com

YouTube is like a giant virtual amusement park where you can ride all sorts of wild and wacky roller coasters of entertainment. From cute cat videos to mind-bending science experiments, YouTube has something for everyone. It's a place where you can go down the rabbit hole of your interests and emerge hours later with a newfound appreciation for the weird and wonderful world we live in. Plus, there's always a chance you'll stumble upon the next viral sensation and become an internet superstar yourself! So buckle up and get ready for the ride of your life on YouTube!

REPETITION

Disney+

www.disneyplus.com

Disney+ is like a magical portal that transports you straight to the happiest place on Earth, without the long lines and overpriced churros. It's a streaming service that combines all your favorite classic Disney films with new and exciting original content, like The Mandalorian and WandaVision. You can join Elsa on her journey to find herself in Frozen, or follow the adventures of the Avengers as they save the world from evil villains. Plus, with the added bonus of being able to watch all the Disney Channel shows from your childhood, you can relive those awkward teenage years with the cast of Lizzie McGuire. So grab some popcorn, snuggle up in your favorite Disney-themed onesie, and let the magic of Disney+ whisk you away to a world of pure imagination!

CONSISTENCY

Instagram

www.instagram.com

Instagram is like a virtual scrapbook where you can curate your life's greatest moments and share them with the world (or just your followers, if you prefer). It's a place where you can show off your latest #OOTD, post mouth-watering food pics, and humblebrag about your latest accomplishments. But it's not just about self-promotion; Instagram is also a platform for discovering new trends, connecting with friends, and finding inspiration from people all over the world. And let's be real, who doesn't love scrolling through adorable puppy pics and hilarious memes? So whether you're an aspiring influencer or just here for the dopamine hit of those sweet, sweet likes, Instagram is the perfect place to showcase your personality and creativity in a fun and visual way.
