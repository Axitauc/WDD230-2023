Lima, Peru's Capital

Chamber of Commerce

The Lima Chamber of Commerce is a dynamic organization that serves as a bridge between the business community and the government in Peru's capital city. It is a non-profit entity that promotes economic growth, facilitates trade and investment, and provides a platform for networking, collaboration, and advocacy among its members. The Chamber of Commerce plays a vital role in promoting a business-friendly environment and fostering entrepreneurship in Lima, making it a key player in the city's economic development.
